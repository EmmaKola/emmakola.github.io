Nova Med Solutions

Transforming pharmaceutical data into actionable intelligence

Project Overview

This project took a deep dive into Nova Med Solutions, a dynamic and leading pharmaceutical company operating across Europe and globally.

The goal is to optimise sales performance, improve inventory management, and uncover key business insights using data.

About the Business

NovaMed Solutions is a pharmaceutical distributor with strong global presence, known for high standards and reliable supply to healthcare providers.

The company supports hospitals, distributors, and direct users with data-driven decision-making and operational efficiency.

Business Problems

  • Limited Sales Performance Visibility
  • Poor Product Performance Tracking
  • Insufficient Customer Insights
  • Lack of Regional Market Visibility

Project Objectives

  • Identify top-performing customer segments
  • Analyse profit margins across products and regions
  • Detect historical sales patterns for forecasting
  • Understand key customer demographics
  • Develop an interactive dashboard for decision-making
  • Provide actionable business recommendations

Data Analysis Process

  1. Data Collection: Data sourced and validated from internal systems
  2. Data Cleaning: Removed duplicates, inconsistencies, and outliers
  3. ETL Process: Transformed and loaded data into Power BI
  4. Data Modelling: Built relationships between tables
  5. Calculations: Created measures and KPIs
  6. Dashboard Creation: Built interactive visuals and reports

Executive Summary

This project delivers data-driven insights to improve revenue growth, optimise inventory, and enhance customer engagement.

Key Insights

Revenue shows cyclical growth across months.

Recommendations

  • Focus on high-performing segments
  • Improve seasonal inventory planning
